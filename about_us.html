Connect Wellard is a community group formed to connect people together as well as computers to create both professional and physical networks.


Since 1st Aug 2018, we've met weekly on Wednesdays at the John Wellard Community Centre (JWCC).

People are welcome to bring any ideas for new projects that enable people & our technology to collaborate & communicate, or just join us to work on a project to learn something new, or just talk people with similar interests.


The first projects we're making progress on in 2018 have been building infrastructure for community WiFi & IoT communication networks, together with hardware & software to use those networks. We've several WiFi access points, a LoRaWAN gateway, a few 'smart' sensors and DevKits to experiment with.


From Arduino..to..Zigbee there is many technologies our group will work together to learn, for fun and professional career development.
See our Projects page for info about work-in-progress & ideas that need people to work on them!

Connect Wellard is a proud supporter of the Tech Tinkering event, to be held in JWCC on the last Saturday of each month, as a great opportunity to showcase to our local community what we're working on and encourage more people to enjoy new technology.
